## 🔧 SERVICE IMPLEMENTATIONS

### liquidityService.ts
**What it does:** Prevents trades in illiquid penny stocks
**Functions:**
- `checkLiquidity(asset)` → LiquidityMetrics
- `validatePositionSize(asset, dollars)` → {isValid, reason, recommendedMax}
- `checkLiquidityBatch(assets[])` → LiquidityMetrics[]

**Integration:** Stage 4 (Fundamental Quality Gate)

---

### earningsService.ts  
**What it does:** Blocks trades 5 days before and 3 days after earnings
**Functions:**
- `checkEarnings(asset)` → EarningsData
- `getEarningsCalendar(assets[])` → EarningsData[]
- `getAssetsInDangerZone(assets[])` → string[]

**Integration:** Stage 2 (News & Catalyst Screen)

---

### correlationService.ts
**What it does:** Real-time correlation matrix updated every 5 minutes
**Functions:**
- `getCorrelationMatrix(period)` → CorrelationMatrix
- `shouldAddAssetToPortfolio(asset, portfolio)` → {shouldAdd, reason}

**Integration:** Stage 3 (Institutional & Sector Flow), Stage 11 (Risk)

---

### optionsFlowService.ts
**What it does:** Analyzes call/put flow and detects smart money positioning
**Functions:**
- `analyzeOptionsFlow(asset, price)` → OptionsFlow

**Integration:** Stage 10 (Sentiment & Options Intelligence)
